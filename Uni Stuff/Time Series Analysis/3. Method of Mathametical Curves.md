# Method of Mathametical Curves
## Straight Line Trend (Linear Trend)
$$T_t = a +bt, \;\;\; b\neq0$$
## Second Degree Polynomial (Parabolic Trend)
$$T_t = a +bt+ct^2, \;\;\; c\neq0$$
## Exponential Curve 
$$T_t = ab^t, \;\;\; a>0$$
## Modified Exponential Curve 
$$T_t = k+ab^t, \;\;\; k>0$$
## Gompertz 
$$T_t = kab^t, \;\;\; k>0$$
